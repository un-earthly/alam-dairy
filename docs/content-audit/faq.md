# Master FAQ Registry — Alam Dairy

This registry consolidates the standard customer, farmer, and partner questions regarding **Alam Dairy's B2C and B2B operations**.

---

## 1. General & Corporate FAQs
### Q: Where are the Alam Dairy farms located?
**A**: We operate three dedicated farms in Bangladesh: Savar Home Farm (Savar, Dhaka) for milking and processing; Gazipur Pasture (Gazipur) for grazing Sahiwal A2 herds; and Manikganj Fodder Farm (Manikganj) for Napier grass cultivation and livestock quarantine.

### Q: What food safety certifications do you hold?
**A**: Our products carry BSTI (Bangladesh Standards and Testing Institution) licenses, Halal Certification from the Islamic Foundation Bangladesh, and comply with the hygiene standards of the Bangladesh Food Safety Authority.

### Q: Can I visit the farms?
**A**: Yes. We host open farm tours on Friday and Saturday mornings for families, school groups, and farmers. Tours can be booked in advance via our Contact portal.

## 2. B2C Consumer & Subscription FAQs
### Q: Is your milk pasteurized or raw?
**A**: We offer both. Our standard fresh cow milk is pasteurized, bottled, and chilled within hours of milking. Our premium Sahiwal A2 milk can be ordered raw or pasteurized. All options are delivered under a strict cold chain.

### Q: What is A2 milk and why is it premium?
**A**: Most commercial milk contains a mixture of A1 and A2 beta-casein proteins. Our indigenous Sahiwal cow breed naturally produces milk containing only the A2 protein type, which many families find gentler on digestion. We maintain a separate herd and bottle A2 milk separately.

### Q: How do recurring dairy subscriptions work?
**A**: You can select a product (such as Fresh Milk or Mishti Doi) and choose a delivery frequency: daily, weekly, biweekly, or monthly. The system schedules deliveries automatically, and you can pause, cancel, or skip individual cycles at any time from your account dashboard.

### Q: Why does the ghee solidify with a grainy texture?
**A**: A grainy texture is the natural signature of slow-cooked, unadulterated ghee. Because we simmer butterfat over low heat for nine hours without adding artificial crystallization agents or vegetable oils, the lipids naturally form grain structures in cool temperatures.

## 3. B2B Farm Supplies & Livestock FAQs
### Q: Are the cattle sold online healthy and vaccinated?
**A**: Yes. Every head of cattle sold by Alam Dairy is inspected by our resident vet, dewormed, and vaccinated against Foot-and-Mouth Disease (FMD) and anthrax. Buyers receive a complete health certificate and vaccination history card.

### Q: Why is there no "Add to Cart" button for some cattle listings?
**A**: Live livestock purchases require physical inspections, certificate reviews, and transport arrangements. Clicks on cattle listings trigger a "Call for Quote" action to connect you directly with our farm office (`tel:01700000000`).

### Q: Do you offer bulk discounts on animal feeds?
**A**: Yes. We implement volume-based discounting tables. Individual feed listings (e.g., Cattle Feed 50kg, Maize Silage 25kg) display wholesale price tiers, which apply automatically when order quantities exceed threshold limits (e.g., 10+ bags).

## 4. Delivery, Payments & Accounts FAQs
### Q: What areas do you deliver to?
**A**: For fresh B2C dairy products, we deliver same-day to households across metropolitan Dhaka. For B2B farm supplies (feeds, machinery, medicines), we ship nationwide via commercial freight networks.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A**: We support mobile financial services (bKash and Nagad), Cash on Delivery (COD), direct bank transfers, and major credit cards.

### Q: How does the B2B Farmer Credit program work?
**A**: Certified farmers (verified in our database with `is_farmer = true`) can be granted a `credit_limit`. This allows them to checkout feed or equipment on account, with outstanding balances reconciled monthly.
