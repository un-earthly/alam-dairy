# Brand Identity & Copy Guidelines — Alam Dairy

This document defines the voice, messaging pillars, preferred vocabulary, and storytelling style of the **Alam Dairy brand**.

---

## 1. Brand Voice & Tone
The Alam Dairy brand voice is **grounded, honest, transparent, and proud**. It represents three generations of local farming expertise. It balances the warm credibility of a family business with the operational efficiency of a modern delivery service.

* **Warm & Grounded**: Speak as local farmers who care for their animals and soils. Avoid clinical, overly corporate, or detached tones.
* **Honest & Transparent**: Present factual product information. Do not make exaggerated nutritional or medical claims.
* **Proud of Heritage**: Celebrate local agricultural heritage (such as the Sahiwal breed, traditional ghee making, and Madaripur roots).
* **B2B Supportive**: Address fellow farmers as partners, offering support, credit, and reliable inputs.

---

## 2. Core Messaging Pillars
Every piece of copy generated for the platform must align with these four pillars:

1. **Purity Without Shortcuts**:
   * *Copy Angle*: Our milk is never reconstituted, watered down, or treated with chemical extenders.
   * *Core Slogan*: *"Pure milk is a promise, not a product."*
2. **Same-Day Freshness**:
   * *Copy Angle*: The connection between dawn milking and morning breakfast tables.
   * *Core Slogan*: *"Milked at dawn, at your door by breakfast."*
3. **Animal Welfare & Herd Intimacy**:
   * *Copy Angle*: We know our cows, pasture-feed our animals, and ensure weekly veterinary checkups.
   * *Core Slogan*: *"A farm is not a factory. It is a family that happens to have four hundred hooves."*
4. **Circular Sustainability**:
   * *Copy Angle*: Recycling manure to biogas, running chillers on solar power, and using returnable glass bottles.
   * *Core Slogan*: *"Farming that gives back to the land."*

---

## 3. Vocabulary & Style Sheet
To maintain consistency across Bengali and English copy, writers must follow these vocabulary rules:

### Preferred Terms
* **A2 Milk**: Use *"A2 Milk"* or *"Sahiwal A2 Milk"*. Specify that A2 is a natural protein type found in indigenous Sahiwal breeds.
* **Ghee**: Refer to our product as *"Pure Desi Ghee"* or *"Clarified Butter"*. Describe the texture as *"grainy"* (winter signature of slow cooking) and the color as *"golden amber"*.
* **Doi**: Refer to sweet yogurt as *"Mishti Doi"* or *"Traditional Sweet Yogurt"*.
* **Cattle Sales**: Emphasize *"Vet-Inspected"*, *"Vaccination History Card"*, and *"Quarantine Checked"*.
* **Feeds**: Describe as *"Balanced Rations"*, *"High-Protein concentrates"*, and *"Fodder Silage"*.

### Forbidden Terms (Do Not Use)
* Do not use hyper-industrial terms: *"milk manufacturing plant"*, *"processed dairy product"*, *"automated cow storage"*, or *"mass-produced feed"*.
* Do not use hype-filled tech buzzwords: *"disruptive dairy technology"*, *"revolutionary milk algorithms"*, or *"synergistic farm optimization"*.
* Do not use cheapness as a value hook: *"cheapest milk in Dhaka"*, *"discount cattle market"*, or *"budget cows"*. Focus instead on *"honest value"*, *"fair pricing"*, and *"premium yield"*.

## 4. Brand Pitch Variations
* **One-Sentence Pitch**:
  > "From fresh A2 milk to vet-inspected dairy cattle, Alam Dairy delivers pure farm products and agricultural supplies directly from our pastures to Dhaka households and local farmers."
* **One-Paragraph Pitch**:
  > "Founded in 2015 with a single Sahiwal cow and a bicycle in Madaripur, Alam Dairy has grown into a trusted agricultural ecosystem operating three farms. We bridge the gap between rural farms and urban dining tables by delivering unadulterated milk, ghee, and traditional doi under a strict cold chain. Simultaneously, we support the farming community with high-protein feeds, equipment, and certified dairy cattle, ensuring quality is verified at every step."
* **Elevator Pitch**:
  > "At Alam Dairy, we believe that pure milk is a promise, not a product. That’s why we cultivate our own fodder, inspect every animal weekly with resident vets, and deliver our milk within hours of milking. Whether you are a parent seeking pure dairy for your family or a farmer looking for high-yield feeds and certified cattle, we provide traceably pure, halal, and BSTI-licensed farm products direct from the source."
