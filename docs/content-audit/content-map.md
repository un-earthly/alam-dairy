# Content Skeleton Map — Alam Dairy

This map establishes the goals, audiences, required sections, asset parameters, and trust signals for all primary frontend pages on the **Alam Dairy platform**.

---

## 1. Homepage (`/[locale]/`)
* **Goal**: Establish brand authority, route users into correct purchasing lanes (Shop vs. Farm), and build immediate trust.
* **Audience**: Urban families, health-conscious shoppers, and local farmers.
* **Search Intent**: Informational / Navigational.
* **CTAs**: Primary: *"Shop Dairy Products"*; Secondary: *"Browse Farm Supplies"*.
* **Required Sections**:
  1. *Hero Video Panel*: High-contrast overlay, founding badge, primary CTAs.
  2. *Story Teaser*: Md. Alam's founders note, pasture photograph.
  3. *Quick Stats Banner*: Numerical indicators of customer satisfaction and scale.
  4. *Lane Selector*: Visual split cards routing users to B2C Shop or B2B Farm.
  5. *Farm Gallery*: Photographic showcase of pastures and milking parlors.
  6. *How it Works*: Three-step guide (Browse -> Order -> Delivery).
  7. *Featured Products*: Top-selling dairy products showing prices and add buttons.
  8. *Parallax Band*: Scenic visual break featuring the core brand message.
  9. *Trust Badges Grid*: Halal, Fresh Daily, Dhaka Delivery, Vet-Inspected badges.
  10. *Customer Testimonials*: Scrolling carousel of verified buyer quotes.
* **Suggested Assets**: Background hero loop video (`hero-720.mp4`), panoramic pasture image (`pasture-panorama.jpg`), product photos (milk, yogurt, ghee).
* **Trust Signals**: Islamic Foundation Halal badge, BSTI license notice, founder's signature block.

---

## 2. Shop Landing Page (`/[locale]/shop`)
* **Goal**: Drive B2C sales and milk/yogurt subscription signs-ups.
* **Audience**: Consumers in Dhaka.
* **Search Intent**: Transactional.
* **CTAs**: Primary: *"Add to Cart"*; Secondary: *"Subscribe"*.
* **Required Sections**:
  1. *Shop Header*: Localized category descriptors, clean search bar.
  2. *Filters Panel*: Category filters (Milk, Yogurt, Ghee & Butter, Sweets), sorting dropdown (Newest, Price: Low to High, Price: High to Low).
  3. *Product Grid*: Cards displaying rating stars, pricing, units, and tags (e.g., "Best Seller", "Farm Fresh").
* **Suggested Assets**: High-contrast, clean-background pack shots of dairy jars and bottles.
* **Trust Signals**: Verified average rating badges, clear unit markers (e.g., `/litre`, `/500g`).

---

## 3. Farm Supplies Page (`/[locale]/farm`)
* **Goal**: Drive B2B supplies sales and cattle quote requests.
* **Audience**: Dairy farm owners, cattle breeders, and rural smallholders.
* **Search Intent**: Commercial Investigation / Transactional.
* **CTAs**: Primary: *"Add to Cart"* (for feeds/tools); Secondary: *"Request Quote"* (for cattle).
* **Required Sections**:
  1. *Farm Header*: Banner with image of active pastures.
  2. *Category Filters*: Cattle, Animal Feed, Veterinary, Equipment.
  3. *Supplies Grid*: Product cards. Live cattle cards display the *"Call for Quote"* CTA.
* **Suggested Assets**: Photos of live cows in open stalls, machinery close-ups.
* **Trust Signals**: "Vet Inspected" badges on live animal listings, volume pricing indicators.

---

## 4. Product Detail Page (`/[locale]/shop/[slug]`)
* **Goal**: Convert visits into sales or recurring subscriptions.
* **Audience**: Direct consumers.
* **Search Intent**: Transactional.
* **CTAs**: Primary: *"Add to Cart"*; Secondary: *"Subscribe & Save"*.
* **Required Sections**:
  1. *Product Gallery*: Carousel showing multiple product views.
  2. *Meta Info*: Halal status, category, brand name, verified average ratings.
  3. *Purchase Options Card*: Quantity selector, one-time price, subscription frequency toggle (Daily, Weekly, etc.).
  4. *Tabs Section*: Detailed description, technical specifications (fat content, shelf life), and customer reviews.
  5. *Related Products*: Horizontal list of matching cross-sells.
* **Suggested Assets**: Zoomable high-resolution imagery, nutrition fact tables.
* **Trust Signals**: "Verified Purchase" badges next to approved reviews.

---

## 5. Checkout Page (`/[locale]/checkout`)
* **Goal**: Minimize cart abandonment and secure transaction data.
* **Audience**: Shoppers completing purchases.
* **Search Intent**: Transactional.
* **CTAs**: Primary: *"Place Order"*; Secondary: *"Continue Shopping"*.
* **Required Sections**:
  1. *Customer Information Form*: Name, phone validation field, email, address, delivery area dropdown.
  2. *Payment Selector*: Custom toggles for bKash, Nagad, Card, or Cash on Delivery.
  3. *Cart Summary*: Line-item list showing subtotals, delivery fees, and discount lines.
* **Suggested Assets**: Payment partner logos (bKash, Nagad, Visa, Mastercard).
* **Trust Signals**: SSL secure checkout indicators, transparent fee declarations.

---

## 6. Company Pages (`about`, `our-story`, `sustainability`, `certifications`)
* **Goal**: Build brand authority and establish trust.
* **Audience**: Consumers, partner organizations, and agricultural researchers.
* **Search Intent**: Informational.
* **CTAs**: Primary: *"Shop Our Products"*; Secondary: *"Contact Our Farm"*.
* **Required Sections**:
  1. *About Page*: Mission declaration, core company values, profiles of our vets and herdsmen.
  2. *Our Story Page*: Chronological interactive timeline of milestones (2015 to present).
  3. *Sustainability Page*: Columns detailing biogas energy loops, solar power setups, water stewardship, and our 2030 commitments.
  4. *Certifications Page*: Grid displaying copies of Islamic Foundation, BSTI, and Department of Livestock Services compliance certificates.
* **Suggested Assets**: Historic farm photos, biogas plant schema, official certificate scans.
* **Trust Signals**: Signatures of founders and vet staff, government license reference numbers.
